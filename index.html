Principles

A principle is deﬁnite and unchanging whereas a value depends upon opinion and is relative. Gravity is a principle whereas customer-centricity is a value. A value is hard to measure, but a principle can be measured. Our principles deﬁne the behaviour requirements of Carocept and our service partners and we want them to be clear and measurable

Vision & Mission

To be recognized as a competent and reliable Black-Owned mining company showing the world a new and better way to do things.
Our mission is the strategic and ﬁnancial management and the directing of mining operations via partnerships.

Culture
